A few tips on creating screenshots:

  • You can upload more than one screenshot and rearrange them later.
  • Choose interesting situations of the application in action.
  • Make the app's window as small as possible while still being aesthetically pleasing.
  • Crop pixel-perfect with a transparent background (visible to the right of the yellow tab). Haiku's Screenshot app does that automatically for most kind of windows. WonderBrush works nicely for cropping and further editing.
